## Key Insight: Even vs Odd Row

The critical difference is in directions 1, 2, 4, 5 (the diagonal directions):

| Direction | Even Row (y%2==0) | Odd Row (y%2==1) |
|-----------|-------------------|------------------|
| NE (1) | (0, -1) | (1, -1) |
| NW (2) | (-1, -1) | (0, -1) |
| SW (4) | (-1, +1) | (0, +1) |
| SE (5) | (0, +1) | (1, +1) |

East (0) and West (3) are always (1, 0) and (-1, 0).
